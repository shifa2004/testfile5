About Ambulance Drone System

The Ambulance Drone System is an innovative project developed at IIITDM Kurnool to revolutionize emergency medical response. Our system combines drone technology with real-time video streaming to provide faster emergency response and enable remote medical professionals to assess situations before the ambulance arrives.

Key Features:

  • Real-time traffic navigation using advanced routing algorithms
  • Live video feed to medical professionals for remote assessment
  • Two-way communication between emergency responders and doctors
  • Automated traffic clearance alerts
  • Integrated emergency call system
